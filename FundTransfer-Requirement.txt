Project: Fund Transfer Application

BR-001: Allow users to transfer funds between their accounts within the same bank.

Implement different transaction types with varying fees:
BR-002:	a. Standard transfer: $2 fee per transaction.
BR-003:	b. Express transfer: $5 fee per transaction.
BR-004:	c. International transfer: $10 fee per transaction.
	
Apply additional fees for expedited transfers based on the transfer amount:
BR-005:	a. For standard transfers:
BR-006:		i. No additional fee for transfers below $1,000.
BR-007:		ii. 1% fee for transfers of $1,000 or more.
BR-008:	b. For express transfers:
BR-009:		i. 2% fee for transfers below $1,000.
BR-0010:		ii. 3% fee for transfers of $1,000 or more.
BR-0011:	c. No additional fees for international transfers.
	
Implement transfer limits based on user account type:
BR-0012:	a. Savings account: Maximum transfer limit of $5,000 per transaction.
BR-0013:	b. Current account: Maximum transfer limit of $10,000 per transaction.
